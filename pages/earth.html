Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It is the densest planet in the Solar System and the largest of the four terrestrial planets.

Interesting Facts about Earth

  • Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen.
  • Earth has one moon, known as Luna.
  • The surface of Earth is 71% water and 29% land.
  • Earth's rotation period is about 24 hours, which defines the length of a day.
  • A year on Earth (one orbit around the Sun) takes about 365.25 days.
